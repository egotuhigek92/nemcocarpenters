Our Story

26 Years,
One Passion

Nemco Carpenter Inc was founded in 1998 by Neil Emco — a third-generation carpenter who learned his trade from his father in a workshop in rural Vermont. Armed with a set of hand tools, a secondhand table saw, and an unshakeable belief in honest craftsmanship, Neil opened his first workshop out of his garage.


Today, Nemco operates from a 6,000 sq ft purpose-built workshop with a team of 14 skilled carpenters and joiners. We serve residential clients, architects, interior designers and commercial developers — but our approach has never changed: every piece is planned carefully, cut precisely, joined with care, and finished with pride.


We've completed over 1,200 projects ranging from a single handcrafted stool to a complete 12-room home fitout. Every job, regardless of size, gets the same attention to detail that built our reputation.
